About botalsepaisa

Empowering sustainability through digital innovation. Our mission is simple – reward recycling, reduce plastic, and build a cleaner India.

Our Mission

BottleBack India aims to revolutionize the way we view plastic bottles by introducing a deposit-return system powered by QR technology. We’re blending sustainability with incentives to make eco-friendly actions part of everyday life.

What We Do

Scan

Users scan the QR code from any BottleBack-enabled bottle via our web/app platform.

Return

Bottles are dropped off at verified kiosks by users or vendors.

Earn

Get instant cashback to your UPI and track rewards transparently.
