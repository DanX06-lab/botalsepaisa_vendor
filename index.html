Help your city recycle and earn with every bottle returned. Vendors are a key part of our sustainability chain.

What Vendors Do

Scan Returned Bottles

Vendors verify and scan the QR codes on bottles returned by users. This marks the bottle as recycled in our system.

Track and Process Returns

Each scan is tracked, and the vendor receives a reward for confirmed collections and returns.

Request Pickups

If bottles accumulate, vendors can request pickup by the BottleBack team for disposal and credit issuance.
